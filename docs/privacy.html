Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Flight CoPilot and other apps published by Electric Dream Software Ltd (“we”, “our”) are designed to work primarily on-device. We keep this policy concise and clear.

1. Data We Process

  • Sensors: accelerometer, gyroscope, barometer, and magnetometer for in-app readings (e.g., turbulence, heading, cabin pressure).
  • Location: GPS for route tracing / nearest-airport detection (if you grant permission).
  • Flight logs: saved locally on your device.

2. Data Use

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers. Processing happens on your device. Purchases and distribution are provided by Apple.

3. Third Parties

  • Apple: App distribution, in-app purchases, and optional crash diagnostics (if you enable “Share with App Developers” in iOS).

4. Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data.

5. Children

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
